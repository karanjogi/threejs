Particle Flow visualization using Three.js

Plane Z: 0

Steps for interaction

  • To rotate the image left click and move the mouse
  • Right click on the image to pan the cylinder
  • To move the 2D plane for the projection use the slider
  • Press reset to revert to the initial visualization
